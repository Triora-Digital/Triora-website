Our Story

A Restaurant Born from a Love of Australian Land

The Olive & Fig opened in 2017 with a simple conviction: Australia's produce is among the finest in the world, and it deserves a kitchen worthy of it.

We work directly with farmers, foragers, and artisan producers across NSW and Victoria — building relationships that shape our menu every single season. What's ripe guides what's on the plate.

Our dining room in Surry Hills seats 58 guests in a warm, unhurried atmosphere. Whether you're joining us for a Tuesday dinner or a milestone celebration, we want you to feel that this meal was made for you.

Meet the Chef

Isabelle Ng

Head Chef & Co-Founder

Isabelle spent a decade cooking in Lyon, Copenhagen, and Melbourne before returning to Sydney to open The Olive & Fig with her partner Thomas in 2017.

Her cooking is rooted in classical French technique, but her heart is entirely Australian. She's obsessed with the Indigenous ingredients of this continent — the wattleed, finger lime, Warrigal greens — and finding ways to weave them into a contemporary menu without gimmick.

Isabelle visits the Flemington markets every Thursday before the restaurant opens. She tastes before she plans. The menu follows.
