Our Technology

Better Technology.
Better Outcomes.

We invest heavily in the latest dental technology — not for appearances, but because it genuinely leads to more accurate diagnoses, less invasive treatments, and faster recovery times for our patients.

🔬

Digital X-Rays

Up to 90% less radiation than traditional X-rays. Instant, high-resolution images that allow us to spot problems early and show you exactly what we're seeing.

🧊

3D CBCT Scanning

Cone-beam CT scanning gives us a precise 3D map of your teeth, jaw, and sinuses — essential for implant planning, orthodontic assessment, and complex extractions.

🏺

CEREC Same-Day Crowns

Using CAD/CAM technology, we design, mill, and fit precision ceramic crowns in a single appointment — no temporaries, no second visits, no waiting weeks.

📷

Intraoral Cameras

A tiny HD camera shows you exactly what's happening inside your mouth on a chairside screen. No more imagining — you see what we see, in real time.

💻

Digital Smile Design

Using DSD software, we can show you a realistic simulation of your new smile before any treatment begins — so you know exactly what to expect.

😴

Happy Gas (Nitrous Oxide)

Nitrous oxide sedation is safe, effective, and wears off within minutes. Available for anxious patients of all ages — just let us know when booking.

FAQ

Your Questions Answered

Everything patients commonly ask before booking. Can't find yours? Just give us a call.

Do you see new patients?

Yes — we welcome new patients of all ages. You can book online or call us directly. For your first visit, we'll schedule a comprehensive examination including digital X-rays to get a complete picture of your dental health.

What should I expect at my first appointment?

Your first appointment is a comprehensive check-up (30–45 minutes). We'll take digital X-rays, examine your teeth and gums, discuss your dental history and goals, and provide a written treatment plan with pricing if any treatment is recommended. Nothing will be done without your consent.

Can I use my health fund?

Yes. We have a HICAPS terminal which means we can process your health fund claim on the spot — you only pay the gap. We accept all major health funds including Medibank, Bupa, HCF, NIB, CBHS, AHM, HBF, and more.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. For treatments over $500, we offer Afterpay (up to $2,000 interest-free) and in-practice payment plans for larger treatments like implants and Invisalign. We believe cost should never be a barrier to good dental health.

I'm very anxious about dental visits. Can you help?

Absolutely. We see many anxious patients and our approach is always calm, patient, and unhurried. We offer Happy Gas (nitrous oxide) which is safe, effective, and wears off quickly. Simply mention your anxiety when booking and we'll make sure everything is tailored to your comfort.

How often should I have a check-up and clean?

We generally recommend every 6 months for patients with healthy gums and good oral hygiene. Some patients may need more frequent visits (every 3–4 months) if they have gum disease or a higher cavity risk. Your dentist will advise you at your appointment.

Can my children come to Elevate Dental?

Yes! We love seeing young patients. We're registered under the Child Dental Benefits Schedule (Medicare), which provides up to $1,095 in free dental treatment for eligible children aged 2–17. We recommend children first visit the dentist around their first birthday.

What are your opening hours?

Mon–Fri: 8:00am–6:00pm (with early 7am appointments on Tues & Thurs). Saturday: 8:00am–2:00pm. Closed Sundays. For dental emergencies outside hours, please call our emergency line and leave a message — a dentist will call you back.
