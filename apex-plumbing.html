FAQ

Answers Before You Call

The questions we get asked most. Can't find what you need? Just give us a ring.

Do you charge call-out fees?

For standard bookings during business hours, there's no call-out fee — you pay only for the work carried out. After-hours and emergency call-outs attract a fixed after-hours fee which we'll always tell you upfront before dispatch.

How quickly can you get to me in an emergency?

For emergencies (flooding, burst pipes, gas leaks), our average response time across Sydney is under 60 minutes. We dispatch the closest available tradie to your location the moment you call.

Do I need to be home during the job?

For most internal plumbing jobs, yes — someone 18+ needs to be on the premises. For external drainage and stormwater work, we can sometimes proceed with key/access arrangements. We'll discuss your specific situation when you book.

What hot water brands do you supply and install?

We supply and install all major brands including Rheem, Rinnai, Dux, Vulcan, AquaMax, Bosch, Stiebel Eltron, and Chromagen. We can advise on the best unit for your household size, gas/electric preference, and budget.

Are you licensed for gas work?

Yes. All our gas fitters hold current gasfitter licences issued under NSW Fair Trading. We can carry out all gas work including new installations, appliance connections, pressure testing, and issue gas compliance certificates.

Do you do body corporate / strata work?

Yes — we work with strata managers, property managers, and body corporates across Sydney. We can provide detailed job reports, compliance documentation, and work to strata-approved budget limits when required.

What does your 12-month guarantee cover?

Our guarantee covers all workmanship for 12 months from completion. If a fault is directly caused by our installation or repair, we'll return and fix it at no charge. This is in addition to any manufacturer warranties on parts and equipment.

How do I pay, and do you offer payment plans?

We accept cash, card (Visa/Mastercard), bank transfer, and EFTPOS on the day. For larger jobs over $2,000, we can arrange staged payment schedules. We do not accept payment before work is started — payment is on completion.
